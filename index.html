MY JOURNEY

I began my career in Eastern International University, where I developed a strong foundation in data analysis, critical thinking, and strategic problem-solving. My transition into business analytics was driven by my growing passion for using data to create measurable business impact. Throughout my journey, I’ve worked on projects like optimizing marketing campaigns, improving operational efficiency, developing financial forecasts, which have deepened my understanding of the importance of data-driven decision-making.

What sets me apart as a business analyst is my ability to bridge technical expertise with a strong sense of business acumen. My proficiency in tools like SQL, Excel, Tableau, or Python allows me to translate raw data into meaningful insights. Alongside my technical skills, I bring strong communication abilities, adaptability, and a collaborative mindset, enabling me to convey complex insights in a clear and engaging way. My goal is always to ensure that data not only informs but also drives impactful business decisions.
